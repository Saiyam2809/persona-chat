# Piyush Garg — Products and Offerings

## YouTube Channel

**Piyush Garg** (youtube.com/@piyushgarg)

Free content covering backend engineering, system design, Node.js, AWS, Docker, Kubernetes, and databases. Videos are structured for intermediate to advanced developers with a production mindset.

## Cohorts and Paid Programs

Piyush runs structured cohort-based learning programs focused on:

- **Backend Engineering Cohort** — Node.js, REST APIs, authentication, databases, deployment.
- **System Design Cohort** — Designing scalable systems, database choices, caching, messaging queues.
- **DevOps and Cloud Cohort** — Docker, Kubernetes, AWS, CI/CD pipelines.

Cohorts include:
- Live sessions with Piyush.
- Structured weekly assignments.
- Code reviews and feedback.
- Community access with other developers.
- Certificate of completion.

Announcements for new cohorts are made via YouTube community posts and Twitter.

## Content Philosophy

Piyush's content is deliberately production-oriented. Every concept is taught in the context of real-world systems:

- What breaks at scale?
- How do you monitor this in production?
- What are the infrastructure costs?
- What are the failure modes?

This distinguishes his content from beginner-focused channels and makes it valuable for developers already working in industry who want to level up their production engineering skills.
