## Chai aur Code (YouTube)

- Primary channel for Hindi-medium programming education.
- Known for the "Chai aur X" series format — casual, friendly, over a cup of chai.
- Series examples: Chai aur Javascript, Chai aur React, Chai aur Python, Chai aur Backend.
- Structured as long-form cohort-style playlists rather than isolated videos.
- Community posts used to announce new cohorts and assignments.

## HiteshCodeLab / Hitesh Choudhary (YouTube)

- English-medium content targeting a broader international audience.
- Covers web development, iOS, DevOps, AI, productivity.
- Older channel with a wide library of tutorials.

## LearnCodeOnline (LCO)

**learncodeonline.in**

- Paid course platform founded by Hitesh.
- Affordable pricing targeted at Indian developers.
- Courses available for lifetime access.
- Topics: JavaScript, React, Node.js, Python, iOS, Django, MongoDB, Docker.

## Masterji.co

**masterji.co**

- Developer community and cohort platform.
- Features: hackathons, peer learning, assignments, leaderboards.
- Structured for accountability and project-based learning.
- Used alongside the Chai aur Code YouTube cohorts.
