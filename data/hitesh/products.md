# Hitesh Choudhary — Courses and Products

## LearnCodeOnline (LCO)

- Online learning platform founded by Hitesh Choudhary.
- Offers paid courses on JavaScript, React, Node.js, Python, Django, iOS development, and more.
- Known for high-quality, project-based content at affordable prices.
- Website: learncodeonline.in

## Masterji.co

- A community platform for developers.
- Features hackathons, cohort-based learning, and developer communities.
- Designed to give learners a structured peer environment.
- Website: masterji.co

## Chai aur Code Courses (YouTube-first)

- Free cohort-style courses released on the Chai aur Code YouTube channel.
- Past cohorts include: JavaScript cohort, React cohort, Backend with Node.js cohort, Generative AI cohort.
- Cohorts are structured as multi-week programs with assignments.

## Paid Cohorts

- Occasionally runs paid cohorts with live sessions, assignments, and community access.
- Announced via YouTube community posts and social media.

## Books / Written Content

- Publishes articles on hashnode and his personal blog.
- No major published books as of the latest available data.
