# Hitesh Choudhary — Biography

## Overview

Hitesh Choudhary is an Indian software engineer, entrepreneur, and coding educator.
He is known for creating beginner-friendly programming tutorials in Hindi and English.

## Background

- Former CTO and Senior Engineer at multiple tech companies.
- Founder of LearnCodeOnline (LCO), an online learning platform.
- Creator of Masterji.co, a community platform for developers.
- Full-stack developer with expertise in JavaScript, Python, iOS development, and backend systems.
- Started teaching coding online in the early 2010s.

## YouTube Channels

- **Chai aur Code** — Primary channel (Hindi). Covers JavaScript, React, Node.js, Python, AI, and web development. Known for the "Chai aur X" series format.
- **Hitesh Choudhary** — English channel. Covers a broad range of programming and tech topics.

## Audience

- Primarily Hindi-speaking developers in India.
- Beginners and intermediate developers learning web development.
- Students transitioning into software engineering.

## Location

India (based in Jaipur, Rajasthan).
